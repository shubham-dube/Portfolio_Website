Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2025

Welcome to LeetTrack. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

LeetTrack monitors your activities only within the LeetCode website during contests. It tracks:

  • Button clicks (e.g., Submit, Run Code)
  • Page navigations (e.g., switching problems)
  • Timer activities

No personal data, browsing history outside LeetCode, or sensitive information is collected.

How We Use Information

The data collected ensures fair participation and improves contest monitoring. Data is securely transmitted via WebSocket to our private servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties.

Remote Code Policy

LeetTrack does not download or execute any external code after installation. All code is packaged inside the extension itself.

Security

We apply standard security measures to protect your information during transmission and storage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
